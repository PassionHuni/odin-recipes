Raw Vegan Lasagna

Ingredients

Lasagna

  • 2 pcs Zucchini halved & sliced
  • 1 cup Sun-dried Tomato Pesto
  • 2 cups Homemade Macadamia Ricotta
  • Fresh Basil Leaves to taste
  • 6 Cherry Tomatoes halved

Macadamia Ricotta

  • 2 cups Macadamias soaked
  • 1 cup Water
  • 2 tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 Lemon Zest
  • 1 tsp Sea Salt

Directions

Raw Ricotta

  1. Combine everything in a food processor until creamy smooth.
  2. Taste and add any ingredient you feel it needs more of: lemon juice, nutritional yeast, etc.
  3. chill in refrigerator.

Lasagna

  1. Using a mandoline, shave zucchini lengthwise into thin slices and cut into 4 inch lengths.
  2. Brush the zucchini lightly with olive oil and season with a pinch of sea salt.
  3. Cut the cherry tomatoes in half.
  4. Spoon dollops of sun-dried tomato pesto, ricotta and pesto ( a small spoonful of each) on top of each layered piece of seasoned zucchini in a small rectangle shape.
  5. Repeat two more layers with sun-dried tomato pesto, ricotta, tomato slices, and herbs.
  6. Garnish with basil and serve.
