A RAW FLOURLESS PIZZA CRUST RECIPE, USING GLUTEN-FREE SPROUTED BUCKWHEAT

A sprouted pizza crust with raw marinara sauce, pesto and vegan seed cheese

Ingredients

Pizza Crust

  • 4C buckwheat sprouts (sprouted from 2C dry buckwheat)
  • 3C chopped carrots
  • 1C ground chia seeds
  • 4T coconut oil
  • 2T Italian herbs
  • 1t Celtic sea salt or pink salt

Directions

  1. Prepare buckwheat sprouts using the instructions above.
  2. Grind whole chia seeds to create a fine powder.
  3. Using a food processor, grind down the buckwheat sprouts (with salt, and herbs) until it forms a dough ball (this may take a minute or two); set aside in a bowl.
  4. Process the chopped carrots into a fine consistency.
  5. Mix by hand or process all ingredients (dough, carrots, chia and coconut oil) until thoroughly incorporated.

  6. Place 1C portions onto a dehydrator non-stick sheet with a screen underneath for support. One crust per tray, four trays total, when using an Excalibur dehydrator.
  7. Flatten out into a rounded shape (about 1/8-1/4" thick) with a rolling pin or wet hands.
  8. Form a raised edge by lifting up and around the crust exterior. (optional)
  9. Dehydrate on medium heat setting for approximately 6-8 hours.
  10. After 3 hours remove non-stick sheet lining and keep pizza facing upright on the screen only.
  11. Continue to dehydrate until a dry, yet flexible crust-like texture is attained.
